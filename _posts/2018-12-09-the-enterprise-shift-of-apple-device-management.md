## Defining "Enterprise"

To start, we need the foundation of a consistent understanding of "Enterprise" and I suggest we view it from this perspective:

> Software or Hardware tools _provided by employers_ that _enable_ employees to _do their jobs_.

Following the emphasized lines above, Enterprise technology is: managed, integrated, and powerful.
