Enterprise Shift in Apple Device Management

macOS, iOS, tvOS

This is the story of how the iPhone and iPad have pushed Apple forward in their Enterprise management endeavors across their platforms.

  • Not long into the the 2018-2019 school year I got an email about speaking at the TCEA Technology Leadership Summit that December. Call for proposals had ended quite some time earlier, but they were looking for different perspectives: especially those dealing with device management. My estimation is that TCEA blasted this email out to most or all of their members - and I was one who responded. Regardless of the process, I'm pulling slides from that presentation for this post - let's take a journey through the past decade or so of Apple device management.
